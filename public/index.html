Social Speaking

A game for socializing and bonding


Every social interaction includes meaningful dialogue. As a conversation moves forward, the richness of what's being said easily gets lost. This is a game that visualizes the words each individual says in a way that's interactive and fun.

As an individual talks, his/her words are translated to text and visualized in a terrain of dialogue. There's one rule to this game— don't get hit by what people say.




NOTES:
Use keyboard arrow keys as controls
Use Google Chrome
Allow microphone access
